# About

## Who We Are

CustomSong helps people turn personal memories into custom songs, music videos, and printable keepsakes. We build tools for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, family milestones, and the moments that are hard to capture with ordinary messages.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to make personalized music gifting fast, emotional, and accessible. A great song should feel specific to the person receiving it, even when it starts from a few simple notes and memories.

## What We Do

We focus on three gift formats:

- **Custom Songs**: Turn stories, names, dates, and messages into studio-style personalized tracks.
- **Music Video Gifts**: Pair the song with photos and lyric scenes for a shareable keepsake.
- **Printable Wall Art**: Transform song lyrics into downloadable posters and layouts.

## Our Values

### Quality
We care about clear audio, meaningful lyrics, and a polished gift experience.

### Privacy
Personal stories deserve careful handling and simple, private sharing options.

### Creativity
We use AI to help people express something personal, not to make every gift sound the same.

### Reliability
We keep the workflow simple so last-minute gifts can still feel thoughtful.

## Why Choose Us

- **Fast previews**: Create a personalized song sample in minutes.
- **Gift-ready bundle**: Unlock a full song with optional video and wall art tools.
- **Flexible editing**: Refine lyrics and gift details before sharing.
- **Simple delivery**: Send privately or download the finished keepsake.
