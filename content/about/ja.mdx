# About

## Who We Are

CustomSong helps people turn personal memories into custom songs, music videos, and printable keepsakes. The public site currently ships in English only, so this file is kept as a safe fallback rather than a separate localized page.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to make personalized music gifting fast, emotional, and accessible.

## What We Do

We focus on three gift formats:

- **Custom Songs**: Personalized tracks based on names, dates, stories, and messages.
- **Music Video Gifts**: Shareable videos with photos, lyrics, and emotional scenes.
- **Printable Wall Art**: Downloadable lyric posters and keepsake layouts.
